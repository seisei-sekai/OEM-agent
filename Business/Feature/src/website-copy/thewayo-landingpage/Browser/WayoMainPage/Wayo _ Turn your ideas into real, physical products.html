Ask Nory AI

Wayo is an AI-powered platform for creating real, physical products – from branded merch to fully custom builds.


We help teams design, source, produce, and deliver products using AI tools and a vetted global manufacturing and logistics network. By cutting out traditional middlemen, Wayo is able to offer 30-50% lower prices, fast production in as little as two weeks, and low minimums starting at just 20-100 units.

You can create everything from ready-to-customize branded merch (like apparel, bags, drinkware, and accessories) to fully custom products made from scratch. Wayo supports startups, brands, teams, and creators making anything from swag and product drops to promotional products and retail-ready goods.

Wayo helps you go from idea to finished product without having to manage suppliers, factories, or logistics yourself.


You can start by customizing a ready-made product from our catalog or by sharing an idea for something fully custom. While you can browse the catalog on your own, Nory, our AI sourcing agent, makes the process easier by recommending the right products, creating production-ready mockups, and helping match your project with the right factory.


Once you approve pricing and samples, the Wayo team takes care of production, quality checks, and delivery. You get clear timelines, full visibility at every step, and one dashboard to manage everything.

Wayo is built to remove the tradeoffs teams usually face when making physical products.


Unlike Alibaba-style sourcing, Wayo is easy to use and fully managed. We work only with vetted factories, handle quality control, and support lower minimums—so teams don’t have to take on risk, complexity, or guesswork themselves.


Unlike print-on-demand and traditional promotional product platforms, Wayo offers factory-direct pricing, better materials, and far more flexibility. You’re not limited to basic templates or narrow customization methods – you can create cooler, higher-quality products that actually feel custom, or source something entirely from scratch.

Nory is Wayo’s AI Sourcing Agent.


Nory helps teams move from idea to production faster by recommending the right products, creating production-ready branded mockups, and guiding sourcing decisions based on your goals, budget, and timeline. Working alongside Wayo’s team, Nory reduces back-and-forth and helps projects get into production with clarity and confidence.
